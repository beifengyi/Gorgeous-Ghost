As a scholar who strays into Lanruo Temple, you will encounter the stunning female ghost Nie Xiaoqian. With the conspiracy of the tree demon Granny and the help of the warrior Yan Chixia, you will write a love story between human and ghost that transcends life and death. Your choices will directly affect the development of the story.
